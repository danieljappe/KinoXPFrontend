    • Welcome to Kino XP

    Your ultimate film haven in the heart of the city! We are dedicated to providing the best cinema experience to our customers and creating unforgettable moments on the big screen.

    Our History

    Kino XP has a rich history that spans decades. From our humble beginnings as a classic cinema in the city center, we have grown into a modern cinema destination. Over the years, we have consistently adapted to the latest technological advancements and trends in the film industry to ensure that our visitors have the best possible film experience.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to create a space where film enthusiasts of all ages can immerse themselves in the magic of cinema. We believe in the power of film to entertain, inspire, and connect people, and we are proud to provide our community with a platform to experience this magic. We work constantly to expand our film selection, improve the technology in our cinemas, and create an atmosphere that feels like a second home for our visitors.

    Our Dedicated Team

    Behind Kino XP is a dedicated team of passionate professionals who are passionate about film and hospitality. Our employees are here to ensure that your cinema experience is exceptional. From our friendly ticket office staff to our technicians working behind the scenes, we are all focused on providing the best possible service.

    Our Cinema

    Our cinema is equipped with state-of-the-art technology, including high-definition screens and crystal-clear surround sound. We have different cinema halls to cater to different tastes and preferences, whether you prefer the latest blockbuster films, art films, or family entertainment.

    Join Us

    We invite you to step into our cinemas and be a part of our history. Whether you are a frequent guest or visiting us for the first time, you are part of the Kino XP family. Thank you for choosing us for your film experiences, and we look forward to creating unforgettable moments with you.
