Krav:
  • It is a smaller cinema with 2 movie theatres.
  • They are showing different categories of movies e.g., Horror, Romance, Action, Science Fiction, etc.
  • There are different age limits for the individual movies.
  • There are 20 rows with 12 numbered seats per row in the smallest theatre.
  • There are 25 rows with 16 numbered seats per row in the largest theatre.
  • At the moment, all reservations of tickets are done by phone.
  • What to show, and in which theatre is planned 3 months in advance.
  • Sometimes extra shows are done.
  • If a movie is doing poorly, it is taken off the program before time.
    • Employees:
  • 2 employees handle the reservations and the sale of tickets, they also manage the sale of popcorn, candy, soda, etc.
  • 1 movie operator
  • 1 employee to inspect the tickets and handle the cleaning
